A column stacks its children vertically. Exactly the same class grammar as fs-row, and every suffix a row accepts a column accepts too — chain them onto the name with dots.
Write this To get this
fs-columnChildren stack top to bottom, each as wide as the container
.gap-none .gap-xs .gap-sm .gap-md .gap-lgSpace between children: 0, 5, 10, 20 or 40px
.align-start .align-center .align-end .align-baseline .align-normalWhere children sit horizontally
.justify-start .justify-center .justify-end .justify-around .justify-between .justify-evenlyHow leftover vertical space is shared out
The axes swap compared with a row. On a column, .align-* moves children left and right, and .justify-* moves them up and down.
