Every layout class uses the same five steps for spacing. .gap-* spaces children in both directions. When you want to control one direction only, reach for .row-gap-* or .column-gap-* instead.

The names describe the gap, not the direction of the layout. On a wrapping row, .row-gap-* is the space between the rows of items (vertical), and .column-gap-* is the space between the items in a row (horizontal). The three galleries below use identical markup.
