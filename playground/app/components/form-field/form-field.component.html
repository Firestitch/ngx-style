Angular Material reserves a strip of space under every form field for hint and error text, whether or not there is any. These classes reshape that strip so fields line up the way you want.
Write this To get this
form-field-padlessRemoves the reserved space under the field
form-field-padless-bottomThe same thing, under a different name
form-field-linelessHides the outline until the field is focused
full-widthStretches the field to its container
No hint With hint The reserved space is now used

Default Padless Padless with hint Hint still renders
}
form-field-padless and form-field-padless-bottom do exactly the same thing. Two names, one behaviour — pick whichever reads better where you use it.
